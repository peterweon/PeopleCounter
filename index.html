How To Play

1. If you guess the number of people in the photo correctly: earn 15 cents!

2. If your guess is incorrect, but it is within 10% of the correct answer: earn 5 cents!

3. If you complete the whole game; earn 10 cents!

What Counts As A Person?

If face/part of head is recognizable (i.e. eyes, nose, lips, ears, chin), it counts as a person.

Animals/pets don't count.

Objects don't count either.

Have fun and best of luck!
